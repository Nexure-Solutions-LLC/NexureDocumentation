## Changing Nameservers with Popular Domain Providers

### Namecheap
1. Log in to your [Namecheap account](https://www.namecheap.com).
2. Navigate to **Domain List** and select your domain.
3. Click **Manage** and find the **Nameservers** section.
4. Select **Custom DNS** and enter the Nexure Solutions nameservers.
5. Click **Save** and wait for propagation.

### GoDaddy
1. Sign in to [GoDaddy](https://www.godaddy.com/).
2. Navigate to **My Products** > **Domains**.
3. Select your domain and click **Manage DNS**.
4. Locate the **Nameservers** section and click **Change**.
5. Select **Enter my own nameservers**, input Nexure Solutions nameservers, and save changes.

### Network Solutions
1. Log in to [Network Solutions](https://www.networksolutions.com).
2. Select **Manage Domains** and find your domain.
3. Click **Edit Nameservers** and input Nexure Solutions nameservers.
4. Save changes and allow time for propagation.

### Squarespace
1. Sign in to [Squarespace](https://www.squarespace.com/).
2. Go to **Domains** and select your domain.
3. Navigate to **Nameservers & DNS Settings**.
4. Select **Use Custom Nameservers** and enter Nexure Solutions nameservers.
5. Save changes and wait for DNS propagation.

### Other Domain Registrars
Most domain registrars follow similar steps:
- Log in to your domain provider's website.
- Navigate to **Domain Management**.
- Locate **Nameservers Settings**.
- Choose **Custom DNS** and enter Nexure Solutions nameservers.
- Save and allow up to 24 hours for changes to take effect.
